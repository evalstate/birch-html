Scoring & provenance

Formula and caveats

Formula: 0.30 × generation_score + 0.55 × checker_score + 0.15 × hygiene_score.
generation_score = generated_ok / total_evals (both 4/4 = 1.0).
checker_score = max(0, 1 − 0.20×failures − 0.03×warnings).
hygiene_score = artifact compliance after unsupported classes / external assets / missing CSS penalties.

Caveats: Provider token schemas differ (cached/reasoning vs effective tokens not apples-to-apples). Checker warnings are mechanical contract signals, not human quality. Wall time includes model latency + harness overhead (small diffs noisy). Same:same screenshots validate geometry/contracts, not aesthetics.
