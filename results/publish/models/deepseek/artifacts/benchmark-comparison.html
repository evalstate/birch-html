Methodology

Scoring formula and run provenance

Scoring formula

Final score = 0.30 · generation_score + 0.55 · checker_score + 0.15 · hygiene_score

  • generation_score = generated_ok / total_evals (pass/fail rate)
  • checker_score = max(0, 1 − 0.20 · checker_failures − 0.03 · checker_warnings)
  • hygiene_score = average artifact compliance after unsupported-classes, external-asset, and missing-CSS penalties

Caveats
  • Provider token schemas differ; cached, reasoning, and effective tokens are not apples-to-apples. Token deltas (±7–8%) should be read as directional, not precise.
  • Checker warnings are mechanical contract signals: they flag structural or rendering deviations but are not a complete human quality score.
  • Wall time includes model latency and harness overhead; small differences (±20 s per eval observed) should be treated as noisy.
  • Hygiene dip of 0.01 may reflect a single artifact deviating on a minor compliance check — not assessed manually.
  • Candidate B’s code-review artifact still needs manual inspection for long diff wrapping before committing to seed promotion.
  • One warning persists on module-explainer (mobile flow caption density) — likely a design constraint, not a regression.
