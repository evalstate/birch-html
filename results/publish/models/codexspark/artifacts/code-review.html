Severity: High (merge safety risk).

Chart builder crashes on sparse/initial fixture data

evals/charts/build_chart_brief.py · previous_latest(), build_svg()

Severity: Medium (CI/runtime reliability).

Evidence: previous_latest() reads weeks[-2] and weeks[-1] without validating list length, and build_svg() indexes by_artifact[artifact][week] directly.

Impact: A fixture with one week of data or missing artifact/week rows will raise IndexError/KeyError and abort the eval job before artifact output is complete.

Recommended fix: guard for len(weeks) < 2, and build SVG points with a missing-week safe path (skip with logged warning or fail with explicit validation).
