Scoring & provenance

Scoring formula

final = 0.30·gen + 0.55·check + 0.15·hygiene
  • gen = generated_ok / total_evals
  • check = max(0, 1 − 0.20·failures − 0.03·warnings)
  • hygiene = avg artifact compliance after penalties for unsupported classes, external assets, missing CSS

Verify: Seed checker = 1 − 0.03·5 = 0.85; Candidate = 1 − 0.03·2 = 0.94. Matches source.json.

Caveats

  • Provider token schemas differ; cached / reasoning / effective tokens are not apples-to-apples — treat the +7.5% token delta as directional.
  • Checker warnings are mechanical contract signals, not a complete human quality score.
  • Same:same screenshot pairs primarily validate geometry & rendering contracts, not aesthetic quality.
  • Wall time includes model latency & harness overhead; per-eval gaps under ~10 s are noise, but the consistent +18–21 s shift is not.
  • n=4 evals, single model, single run — no variance estimate.
