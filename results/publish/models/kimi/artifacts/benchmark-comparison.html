Method

Scoring formula and provenance

Formula

0.30 × generation_score + 0.55 × checker_score + 0.15 × hygiene_score

  • generation_score = generated_ok / total_evals
  • checker_score = max(0, 1 − 0.20 × checker_failures − 0.03 × checker_warnings)
  • hygiene_score = average artifact compliance score after unsupported-classes, external-assets, and missing-CSS penalties

Command

python3 scripts/run_skill_evals.py --skill-dir birch-html --model codexresponses.gpt-5.5 --label skill-candidate-b-gpt55

Caveats

What these numbers do and do not mean

  • Provider token schemas differ; cached, reasoning, and effective tokens are not apples-to-apples.
  • Checker warnings are mechanical contract signals, not a complete human quality score.
  • Same:same screenshot pairs primarily validate geometry and rendering contracts, not aesthetic quality.
  • Wall time includes model latency and harness overhead, so small differences should be treated as noisy.
