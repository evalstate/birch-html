Scoring formula
0.30 * generation_score + 0.55 * checker_score + 0.15 * hygiene_score
  • Generation score = generated_ok / total_evals.
  • Checker score = max(0, 1 - 0.20 * checker_failures - 0.03 * checker_warnings).
  • Hygiene score = average artifact compliance after unsupported classes, external assets, and missing CSS penalties.

Candidate B’s weighted gain is driven mostly by checker_score: 0.85 → 0.94. Hygiene slips slightly: 1.00 → 0.99.

Decision summary

Why promotion is justified

Promote Candidate B because it improves the metric GEPA is optimizing for most directly: final score. The score gain is supported by fewer checker warnings, zero failures, and better per-eval results across all four benchmark slices. The only regressions are operational: more wall time, more tokens, and higher estimated cost.

That tradeoff is acceptable for seed data because the extra spend is bounded and the quality/compliance gains are consistent, not isolated to a single eval. The report still flags one manual follow-up: code-review diff wrapping should be inspected before this seed is locked in.
